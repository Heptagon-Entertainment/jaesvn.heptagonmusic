ABOUT ME
Who is JAE SVN?
JAE SVN is an Argentine singer, rapper, songwriter, and music producer. He first started learning guitar and writing songs at the age of 8. He was always passionate about music, and he quickly developed his abilities for songwriting and guitar playing. In his teenage years, he took an interest in hip-hop and music production. He began to experiment with different styles of music, and he soon developed his own unique sound. As a result of his involvement with music, he created the HEPTAGON project in 2020. Two years later, Jae released his two first songs, "Discord" and "1000 Miles". He's often inspired by personal stories and likes to transform his life experiences into relatable lyrics to comfort people. He strongly believes that music has a lot of power for helping others and make a positive impact on the world.
