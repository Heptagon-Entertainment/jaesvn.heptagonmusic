JAE SVN BLOG
June 30, 2023
"Shine" Digital Release
With an uplifting blend of nostalgic lyrics, electronic elements, and his signature rap style, JAE SVN returns with his new single "Shine".
"The lyrics and the general concept are based on a personal story. However, I also aimed to create a song that people can relate to, just like I did with my previous songs "Discord" and "1000 Miles". This song serves as a "Thank you" to the person who helped me grow and move forward."
"Shine" carries an energetic yet chill vibe, maintaining the same feel as his debut song, "Discord".
"Honestly, I'm still trying to find my own sound. At the same time, I'm eager to experiment with new genres. While having my own style is a really nice thing, I believe it's even better to be open to new possibilities and discover fresh approaches to creating music."
The song is available on all digital platforms, including Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, and more.
